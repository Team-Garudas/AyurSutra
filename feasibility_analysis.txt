### 1.3 TECHNICAL RISK ASSESSMENT

#### 🔴 HIGH RISK AREAS
1. **Video Consultation Stability**: Requires robust testing across devices and networks
2. **Real-time Synchronization**: Complex data consistency challenges
3. **File Upload Performance**: Large medical files may impact performance

#### 🟡 MEDIUM RISK AREAS
1. **Third-party API Dependencies**: Razorpay, Video services reliability
2. **Firebase Vendor Lock-in**: Migration complexity if needed
3. **Mobile Browser Compatibility**: PWA features across different browsers

#### 🟢 LOW RISK AREAS
1. **Basic CRUD Operations**: Well-established patterns
2. **Authentication**: Firebase Auth is battle-tested
3. **UI Components**: shadcn/ui provides solid foundation
